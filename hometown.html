Jakarta!


Jakarta is the capital of Indonesia. It's a big, busy city with lots of people and different cultures. There are important places like a national monument and a big mosque. But sometimes, Jakarta has problems like too much traffic and flooding. People are working hard to make the city better. Jakarta is like the heart of Indonesia, where lots of things happen.
